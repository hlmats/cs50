I wrote a cycle Der Ring des Nibelungen of four operas (Das Rheingold, Die Walküre, Siegfried, Götterdämmerung) for 26 years, from 1848 to 1974. The first performance of the cycle began on August 13, 1976 on Bayreuth Festival.
